Refuse Single-Use Plastic

Today's challenge is to refuse at least one single-use plastic item. This could be a straw, bag, or water bottle. Choose reusable alternatives instead!

Energy Saving

Turning off lights when not in use can save up to 15% of your electricity bill. LED bulbs use 75% less energy than incandescent bulbs.

Water Conservation

A leaky faucet dripping once per second wastes more than 3,000 gallons per year. Fixing leaks can save enough water for 180 showers!

Recycling Facts

Recycling one aluminum can saves enough energy to run a TV for 3 hours. Paper recycling saves 60% energy compared to making paper from raw materials.
